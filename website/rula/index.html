    The Insight
    Paul's two queries look different but they're the same operation at different zoom levels. One slices the flywheel by channel to find underperforming levers. The other decomposes a lever to isolate stakeholder resonance. Both require one thing first: an ontology where Channel, Stakeholder, Motion, and Metric are named, related, and queryable. That's Month 1. Everything else builds on it.
